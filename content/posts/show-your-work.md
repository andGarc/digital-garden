## What now?

1. Go online and post what you're working on right now.

2. Share your work with your colleagues and friends. Use this book as a guide  
share works-in-progress and your curiosities, tell stories, and teach one another. 

## 10 ways

1. You don't have to be a genius 

2. Think process, not product

3. Share something small every day

4. Open up your cabinet of curiosities  

5. Tell good stories 

6. Teach what you know

7. Don't turn into human spam 

8. Learn to take a punch 

9. Sell out

10. Stick around
